📌 Overview

What Are Elections?

A democratic process where citizens choose their representatives through voting

🏛️

Democracy in Action

Elections are the heartbeat of democracy. Think of it like choosing the captain of your team — everyone gets a voice, and the majority decision wins.

🗳️

Your Vote Matters

Every single vote counts. Elections ensure that power belongs to the people, and leaders are accountable to those who elected them.

⚖️

Fair & Transparent

Independent bodies like the Election Commission ensure elections are conducted fairly, without bias or manipulation.

📅

Regular Intervals

Elections happen at regular intervals — typically every 5 years in India — ensuring fresh mandates and democratic renewal.

💡
Did you know?

India conducts the world's largest elections! In the 2024 General Elections, over 960 million voters were eligible to vote across 543 constituencies.
